### **Comprehensive List of Adverbs of How (Manner) Sorted by Popularity with Examples of Usage**

Adverbs of manner describe how something is done. They provide more detail about the action by explaining the way or the method in which it occurs. Below is a comprehensive list of commonly used adverbs of manner, sorted by popularity, along with examples of their usage.

---

### **1. Slowly**

- **Usage**: Describes an action done at a slow pace.
    
- **Example**: _She walked **slowly** to the door._  
    (The walking is done at a slow pace.)
    

---

### **2. Quickly**

- **Usage**: Describes an action done at a fast pace.
    
- **Example**: _He finished the task **quickly**._  
    (The task was completed in a short amount of time.)
    

---

### **3. Carefully**

- **Usage**: Describes an action done with attention to detail or caution.
    
- **Example**: _She drove **carefully** through the storm._  
    (The driving was done with caution due to the storm.)
    

---

### **4. Loudly**

- **Usage**: Describes an action done with a lot of noise.
    
- **Example**: _The children played **loudly** in the park._  
    (The playing was accompanied by a lot of noise.)
    

---

### **5. Softly**
