Here’s a list of 50 adverbs of place (describing **where** something happens) along with usage examples:

1. **Here** – _Please come here and sit down._
2. **There** – _We should meet there in front of the café._
3. **Everywhere** – _I looked everywhere for my keys._
4. **Nowhere** – _I found the document nowhere on my desk._
5. **Somewhere** – _I left my wallet somewhere in the house._
6. **Anywhere** – _You can sit anywhere in the room._
7. **Outside** – _Let's go outside for a walk._
8. **Inside** – _The keys are inside the drawer._
9. **Upstairs** – _I left my phone upstairs in my bedroom._
10. **Downstairs** – _The kitchen is downstairs, on the right._
11. **Above** – _The picture is hanging above the fireplace._
12. **Below** – _There’s a basement below the house._
13. **Near** – _There’s a park near my house._
14. **Far** – _The nearest station is far from here._
15. **Close** – _The supermarket is close to my office._
16. **By** – _She sat by the window during the meeting._
17. **Beside** – _The lamp is beside the sofa._
18. **Around** – _There’s a beautiful garden around the house._
19. **Beyond** – _The mountains lie beyond the horizon._
20. **In front of** – _The car is parked in front of the house._
21. **Behind** – _The garden is behind the house._
22. **Under** – _The cat is hiding under the table._
23. **Over** – _The bird flew over the house._
24. **Across** – _He walked across the street to meet his friend._
25. **Along** – _We walked along the beach in the evening._
26. **Through** – _She walked through the door and into the room._
27. **Into** – _I ran into an old friend at the grocery store._
28. **Out of** – _She jumped out of the car to catch the bus._
29. **To** – _I’m going to the store now._
30. **From** – _She came from the other side of town._
31. **On** – _The book is on the table._
32. **Off** – _The cat jumped off the counter._
33. **In** – _The children are playing in the yard._
34. **At** – _We’ll meet at the restaurant._
35. **Behind** – _The children are playing behind the building._
36. **Near to** – _There’s a library near to my school._
37. **Far away** – _The mountains are far away from here._
38. **Somewhere around** – _I’ve seen him somewhere around here._
39. **Down** – _The river flows down the valley._
40. **Up** – _The road goes up the mountain._
41. **Offshore** – _We went offshore to explore the coral reefs._
42. **Out** – _We went out for a coffee this morning._
43. **Inwards** – _The door opened inwards into the room._
44. **Outwards** – _The door opened outwards into the hallway._
45. **To the left** – _The store is to the left of the park._
46. **To the right** – _The library is to the right of the museum._
47. **Around the corner** – _The bakery is just around the corner._
48. **Inside of** – _The phone is inside of the bag._
49. **At the top** – _The flag is at the top of the pole._
50. **At the bottom** – _The treasure was found at the bottom of the sea._
