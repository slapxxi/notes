### **Comprehensive List of Common Phrasal Verbs with Examples (Sorted by Popularity)**

Phrasal verbs are **verb + particle** combinations that often have meanings different from the individual words. They are essential for natural English communication, especially in **spoken English and interviews**. Below is an extensive list, sorted by popularity and grouped into categories for easier learning.

---

## **1. Most Common Phrasal Verbs (Everyday Use)**

|**Phrasal Verb**|**Meaning**|**Example Sentence**|
|---|---|---|
|**Go on**|Continue|_Please go on with your story._|
|**Come back**|Return|_She'll come back in an hour._|
|**Find out**|Discover information|_I need to find out where he lives._|
|**Give up**|Quit, stop trying|_Don't give up on your dreams._|
|**Look for**|Search|_I'm looking for my keys._|
|**Put on**|Wear clothing|_He put on his jacket._|
|**Take off**|Remove (clothing) / Leave (plane)|_Take off your shoes before entering._|
|**Turn on**|Start a device|_Can you turn on the light?_|
|**Turn off**|Stop a device|_Please turn off the TV._|
|**Wake up**|Stop sleeping|_I wake up at 6 AM every day._|
