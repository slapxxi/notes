### **Comprehensive List of Prepositions of Manner/Method with Examples**

Prepositions of **manner/method** describe **how** something happens or is done. They answer the question **"In what way?"** or **"By what means?"** These prepositions are essential for giving explanations, instructions, and descriptions in both casual and professional speech.

---

## **1. Common Prepositions of Manner/Method**

| **Preposition** | **Usage**                                      | **Example Sentence**                       |
| --------------- | ---------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------ |
| **By**          | Indicates the method used to achieve something | _She traveled **by** train._               |
| **With**        | Indicates the instrument or tool used          | _He cut the paper **with** scissors._      |
| **In**          | Describes the way something is done            | _She spoke **in** a soft voice._           |
| **Like**        | Compares manner to something else              | _He runs **like** a professional athlete._ |
| **As**          | Describes a role or function                   | _She works **as** a teacher._              |

---

## **2. Less Common but Useful Prepositions of Manner/Method**

| **Preposition** | **Usage**                                             | **Example Sentence**                   |
| --------------- | ----------------------------------------------------- | -------------------------------------- |
| **Through**     | Describes the means by which something happens        | _He succeeded **through** hard work._  |
| **Via**         | Specifies a route or method                           | _We sent the package **via** courier._ |
| **Without**     | Describes the absence of something in an action       | _She left **without** saying goodbye._ |
| **On**          | Describes a method of action                          | _She wrote a note **on** paper._       |
| **Over**        | Describes how something is done in an overarching way | _They talked **over** dinner._         |

---

## **3. Advanced Prepositions of Manner/Method**

| **Preposition**  | **Usage**                                                 | **Example Sentence**                                |
| ---------------- | --------------------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------- |
| **Under**        | Describes a condition affecting manner                    | _He spoke **under** pressure._                      |
| **Among**        | Describes action happening within a group                 | _He was respected **among** his peers._             |
| **Against**      | Describes opposition in manner                            | _She fought **against** injustice._                 |
| **Despite**      | Indicates that an action happened regardless of obstacles | _He finished the race **despite** an injury._       |
| **According to** | Describes a method following a rule                       | _We organized the event **according to** the plan._ |

---

## **4. How to Incorporate Prepositions of Manner/Method into Speech**

### **A. Describing How Something Was Done**

- **"She completed the project with great care."**
- **"He won the game through determination."**
- **"They traveled by car to the countryside."**

### **B. Talking About Tools and Methods**

- **"She painted the picture with a fine brush."**
- **"The message was delivered via email."**
- **"He fixed the machine without any help."**

### **C. Comparing Actions**

- **"She danced like a professional."**
- **"He spoke in a polite manner."**
- **"They reacted as expected."**
